# Driscoll's What? So What? Now What? (2007)

**Framework:** Alternative reflection framework — quick and minimal
**Reference:** Driscoll, J. (2007). *Practising Clinical Supervision: A Reflective Approach for Healthcare Professionals*. Baillière Tindall.
**Used by:** Reflection model parallel to Rolfe et al. (2001) used in `lesson/post_lesson_refl.md` (direct cadens). Pragmatic keep — may become standalone tool (`src/tools/reflect_driscoll.ts`).

---

## Overview

Three-question model for rapid reflection. Best suited for 5-minute post-lesson capture when time is limited or the teacher wants to record key observations without full analysis.

---

## The Three Questions

### What? — Vad hände?

**Prompt:**
```
"Beskriv kort: vad hände i lektionen?"
```

Brief factual account. No analysis, no evaluation — just what occurred.

### So What? — Varför spelar det roll?

**Prompt:**
```
"Vad betyder det? Varför är det viktigt?"
```

The interpretive step. Why does what happened matter for student learning? What is the significance of the deviations, surprises, or outcomes?

### Now What? — Vad gör du nu?

**Prompt:**
```
"Vad ska du göra annorlunda nästa gång?"
```

Direct action. No extended analysis — just the most important change.

---

## Example (5 Minutes)

```
VAD? Föreläsningen tog 22 min istället för 15. Diskussionen blev kort.

SÅ VAD? Eleverna fick för lite tid att bearbeta materialet själva.
         Bedömningen visade 30% på analysfrågan — de hann inte öva.

VAD NU? Nästa gång: max 10 min föreläsning, resten aktiv bearbetning.
```

**Output:** A quick note saved with minimal YAML. Can be expanded into a full Gibbs reflection later if needed.

---

## When to Suggest Driscoll

- Teacher says "jag har bara 5 minuter"
- Quick capture between lessons
- When the lesson was routine — no major issues to analyse
- As a stepping stone: "vi kan göra en djupare reflektion senare med Gibbs"

## Differences from Gibbs

| Aspect | Gibbs | Driscoll |
|--------|-------|----------|
| Steps | 6 | 3 |
| Time | 20–40 minutes | 5–10 minutes |
| Depth | Comprehensive | Surface |
| Feelings | Separate step | Not explicitly addressed |
| Theory | Integrated | Not included |
| Output | Full change log | Quick action note |
